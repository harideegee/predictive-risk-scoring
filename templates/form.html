Predictive Risk Scoring

This project leverages advanced machine learning techniques to provide personalized health risk scores based on individual demographic, lifestyle, and clinical data. Using a LightGBM predictive model trained on real-world health data, the system estimates the probability that an individual may have or develop a particular health condition, such as heart disease or related chronic illnesses.

Purpose and Benefits

  • Early Identification: Detect individuals at elevated risk before clinical symptoms appear, enabling earlier interventions and prevention strategies.
  • Personalized Risk Assessment: Tailor healthcare decisions and lifestyle recommendations based on a quantifiable risk score unique to each individual.
  • Resource Optimization: Assist healthcare providers and actuaries in prioritizing patients who may benefit most from monitoring, further testing, or treatment.
  • Data-Driven Decisions: Empower clinicians, insurers, and patients with actionable insights generated from robust statistical models.

Outcomes and Impact

The platform delivers an interpretable risk score on a scale from 0 to 100, representing the likelihood of possessing or developing the target condition. This score serves as a practical tool for improving health outcomes, reducing costly complications, and supporting evidence-based risk management in healthcare and insurance settings.

By integrating data science with actuarial principles, the project exemplifies the future of precision medicine and personalized health risk prediction.
